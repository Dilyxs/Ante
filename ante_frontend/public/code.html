DirectAnswer

Automated, cryptographic verification. Solvers submit decrypted answers. If the answer matches the encrypted message protocol, the closest correct answer wins the bounty instantly via on-chain validation.

The Lifecycle of a Commitment

01 / INITIALIZE

Post & Collateralize

Create a challenge on the Ante program. Users deposit Ante Tokens into a secure, program-owned vault. The conditions for release—be it a hash or a specific voting window—are set at the point of origin.

account_balance_wallet
02 / EXECUTION

Solve & Encrypt

Solvers interact with the challenge. For DirectAnswer bounties, the solution is submitted via an encrypted transaction. Solana's high throughput ensures that "race conditions" are handled with millisecond precision.

lock
03 / FINALITY

Reveal & Verify

Once a solution is matched or approved by participants, the vault triggers an automatic payout. The Ante protocol logs the entire audit trail on the Solana ledger, creating a permanent record of the solution and the verifier.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there fees for posting a bounty? expand_more
Ante charges a 1% protocol fee upon successful resolution of a bounty. There are no fees for posting, though a small amount of SOL is required for account rent on the Solana network, which is refundable upon bounty closure.
How are disputes handled for OpenEnded types? expand_more
For OpenEnded challenges, the winner is determined by participant vote one week after the deadline. If a consensus isn't reached, funds remain in escrow until the designated verifier or DAO provides a final signature.
Can I cancel a bounty once it is posted? expand_more
Bounties can only be canceled after their specified "Freeze Period" has elapsed. This prevents creators from revoking rewards once a solver has already committed resources to finding a solution.
